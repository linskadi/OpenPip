# 英文学术写作规范

## 概述

英文学术写作强调清晰的逻辑结构、精准的语言表达和严谨的论证过程。常用于国际期刊论文、国际竞赛论文以及英文申请文书。

## 核心原则

### 逻辑结构（4S 框架）
- Situation：背景/现状
- Problem：问题/挑战
- Solution：方案/方法
- Significance：意义/结论

### 段落结构（PEEL）
- Point：段落主题句
- Evidence：论据/引用
- Explanation：解释分析
- Link：过渡到下一段

### 句式要点
- 主语明确：This study / The analysis / Table 1 shows...
- 避免模糊：表示程度用具体数据
- 过渡词自然：However, Furthermore, Therefore, In contrast, Consequently

## 常见句式

### 提出观点
- This paper argues that ...
- This study investigates the impact of ...
- The results suggest that ...

### 引用文献
- Previous research has demonstrated that [1]...
- According to Smith et al. [2], ...
- A growing body of evidence suggests that ...

### 描述方法
- The experiment was conducted using ...
- Data were collected from ...
- The model was trained on ...

### 表达结果
- The results indicate that ...
- A significant correlation was found between ...
- Compared to baseline, the proposed method improved ...

### 表达局限
- One limitation of this study is ...
- Further research is needed to ...
- These findings should be interpreted with caution because ...

## 易错点

- a/an/the 冠词使用：首次提及用 a，再次用 the
- 单复数一致：The data are / The phenomenon is
- 避免双重否定
- 逗号不要连接两个完整句子（comma splice）
- 避免 which/that 混用：限定性用 that，非限定用 which

## 引用格式

英文学术写作常用引用格式：
- APA：Author, A. (Year). Title. Journal.
- MLA：Author. "Title." Journal (Year).
- Chicago：Author. "Title." Journal (Year).
